Your Progress Keep up with your latest stats

The following data will assist you in saving money on water bills and tracking others results.

Your Personal Use

This section displays your homes daily water usage for the last week in L/P which gives an indication of your trends.

Track Your Water Supply

This data will allow you to see exactly how full your current water source is, whether that be a dam, lake, river etc.

Know Your Water Restrictions

Based on your current weekly usage, water source levels etc. This section will provide you with a recommended water restriction in order to save you money and keep water sources consistent.
